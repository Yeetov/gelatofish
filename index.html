Team Gelatofish

Our Goals

Team Gelatofish is dedicated to making Discord more fun, creative, and accessible for everyone. We focus on providing features for users without Nitro, supporting customization, and fostering community creativity.

  • Animated profile pictures and dynamic avatars without Nitro.
  • Custom avatar decorations, frames, and banners.
  • Custom badges for community engagement and personalization.
  • Collaborations with projects like UserPFP, Decor, Blackbox, and Obaworkshop.
