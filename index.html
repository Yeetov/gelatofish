Team Gelatofish

Our Goals

Team Gelatofish is a collective of developers and creators working to make Discord a friendlier, more accessible, and fun platform for everyone. We focus on:

  • Providing **animated profile pictures** and custom avatar features for everyone, without requiring Nitro.
  • Creating **custom decorations, banners, and badges** that users can enjoy safely and legally.
  • Supporting the community with tools that **enhance Discord experience** without unnecessary subscriptions.
  • Encouraging **creativity, fun, and accessibility** for all users.

We are proud to partner with projects like UserPFP, Decor, Blackbox, and Obaworkshop to bring free and exciting features to the Discord community.
